20th International Conference on Service-Oriented Computing (ICSOC 2022)

All references to a date mentioned in these terms refer to the Central European Time Zone (CET).

Fees

All fees are stated in EUR currency and include applicable taxes already. Payment is possible by VISA, MasterCard credit cards and bank transfer.

Registrations and publication fee

Conference registration includes access to technical sessions and all conference events. Please note that registration is mandatory for ALL participants, including speakers, session chairs and students. Participants register through our secure on-line registration service. A confirmation email stating your registration details and an invoice will be sent in short to your stated email address.

There should be a different registration for each paper that must be paid by one of the authors. The publication in the proceedings and presentation at the conference is subject to the payment of the registration before September 22nd for regular papers and October 29th for workshops and other types of submissions.

Children under 18 years of age are not permitted to register, enter or attend any of the conference.

Substitution of participants

Substitutions may be made at any time and without any fees. They have to be requested by sending an email to icsoc2022@us.es at least 24 hours before the event begins.

Cancellation / Reimbursement Policy

All refund and cancellation requests must be sent to icsoc2022@us.es. There will be no refund after October 16, 2022, including non-arrivals. Note that if you are an author and cancel your publication fee, the articles included in it will not be presented at the conference nor included in the proceedings.
